🧡 Human

Two torn knees, one shared frustration.

"Recovery shouldn't cost you your independence."

— Richard Hetherington, Co-Founder & CEO, LimbRise

LimbRise started as a 4th-year Sustainable Design Engineering project at UPEI, after Richard tore his knee and Noor tore his meniscus within the same stretch of university. Both lived through the same slow, disconnected road back to feeling normal — crutches that fight your gait, and rehab plans with no feedback loop in between appointments.

That shared experience became the company's thesis: recovery isn't one device or one app. It's a system — a brace that supports natural movement, and software that turns that movement into guidance. We're building both, together.

🔬 Build — Products & Services

One hardware system. One intelligence layer.

RevoStep

A next-generation hands-free mobility brace for knee injuries. RevoStep distributes load directly into the muscle, rather than the arms, helping users stay active through recovery, reduce atrophy, and move with a natural gait while healing.

Reebound
